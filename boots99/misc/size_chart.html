Boot Size Chart

Follow These Steps to Find your Fit

  • 1. Place a ruler flat on the ground.

  • 2. Line up your heel at the end of the ruler. Sometimes it helps to use a flat surface like a wall.

  • 3. Measure your foot to your big toe.

  • 4. Use the chart to calculate your size.

Please Note that every boot and every foot is different. These sizes are approximate.
