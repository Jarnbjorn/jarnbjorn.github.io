Size Chart        SnakeBoots                          Children's Size Chart Here

1.   Place the ruler flat on the ground.

2.   Line up your heel at the end of the ruler.   Sometimes helps to start from a flat surface like a wall.

3.   Measure your foot to your big toe.

4.   Use the chart below to calculate your size.

        Men's Sizes     This is approximate size, every boot and every foot is different
