ATTENTION CALIFORNIANS

Some of Chippewa's boots are regulated by California State Law, which makes shipping certain styles difficult. We'll let you know if there's a style of Chippewa boot on your order that is restricted in your state. Many of these boots CAN be shipped to your door, but at an additional charge and an extended delivery time. Don't worry. We'll contact you and you can make the decision for yourself.
