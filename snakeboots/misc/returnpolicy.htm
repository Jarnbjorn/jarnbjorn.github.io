Merchandise must be unworn and be returned with all the original tags and packaging. You are responsible for the shipping cost of returning the item, but if requesting an exchange, we ship the first exchange at no additional cost if shipping to a street address within the contiguous USA. Additional shipping fees apply to all other locations. If returning for a refund, there is a $15 per pair restocking fee on footwear and a 15% restocking fee on all other items. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Unworn Merchandise: Includes any item that is in new/unworn condition, still contains the original tags, and is in the original packaging. Returns will be returned to you, at your expense, and no refund or exchange can be issued, if the returned item(s) exhibit signs of wear. For footwear, special care must be taken to prevent showing signs of wear on the soles such as dirt, scuffs or marring.  All original packaging and materials, including boxes, must be returned with your merchandise.

Defective Merchandise: If you receive a defect item, contact us at returns@boots99.com and we will provide additional information/instructions for returning the defective item.

Incorrect Merchandise: If you received an item shipped in a different size and/or style than you ordered, contact us at returns@boots99.com  and we will provide additional information/instructions for returning the incorrect merchandise.

Warranty Returns: Most manufacturers warrant their items from flaws in materials and workmanship for a specific length of time. They do not warrant against end-use damage, or improper use and/or care. We offer no warranty beyond that of the manufacturer, but will assist you with your qualifying warranty return. Contact us at returns@boots99.com  for additional information on warranty returns.

PLEASE NOTE: Sizing may vary between manufacturers and styles within the same brand due to cut and/or materials, and in footwear, due to type of leather, toe and/or heel shapes. Minor size variations and/or color variations within a line are not considered flaws in materials or workmanship. The manufacturer/distributor of the merchandise will determine if the item is covered by their warranty.
