Children's Size Chart               Snake Boots

  Order Extra big to allow for growth.  These boots are snakeproof from the bottom to the

top of the boot. But they are not as tall as adult snakeboots. Always use caution around snakes

 especially with children.

Measure from heel out to longest toe with ruler or tape measure.

         Order Sizes:

Children's  13 - 3

   If their foot measures       7 and 1/2 inch = Size 13

   If their foot measures     7 and 3/4 inch = Size 1

   If their foot measures     8 and 1/8 inch = Size 2

   If their foot measures     8 and 3/8 inch = Size 3

 

Boys and Girls  4 - 7

   If their foot measures     8 and 3/4 inch = Size 4

   If their foot measures     9 inches = Size 5

   If their foot measures     9 and 3/8 inch = Size 6

   If their foot measures     9 and 3/4 inch = Size 7
