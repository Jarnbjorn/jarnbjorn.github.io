  Here is the care instructions for Aged Regina, which is a nap, or textured leather.

  For Nubuc or nap leathers, we recommend that you treat this as you would suede.  Use a soft brush or dry cloth to remove the loose dirt.  Then clean the boot with a cleaner recommended for suede.  Also, it is recommended that this type of leather be treated with a water resisting treatment made specifically for suede.
