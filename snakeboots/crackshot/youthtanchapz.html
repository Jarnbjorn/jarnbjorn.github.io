Youth Snake Chaps Khaki Tan

Snake Chapz™ are manufactured with 1000 Denier Cordura™ Nylon encasing polycarbonate inserts and are snake proof from top to bottom. Weighing only 15 ounces each. They are impact tested by the United States Testing Laboratory at ASTM test method F-1342-91 specifications. The material has 1 ounce of urethane waterproofing applied to the inside surface. Made with YKK zipper on the inside of each chap for "easy on - easy off" over boots. Snake Chapz™ are secured in place with a 1" polyester strap, which may either be slipped through a belt loop or over the belt prior to connecting the polycarbonate quick release buckle. This provides "full range" height adjustment - a unique feature with Snake Chapz™
