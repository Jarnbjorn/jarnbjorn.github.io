Welcome Adventurer, to Stadarfell...

Stadarfell is a city beyond the time and space of conventional worlds. The city exists both within and without many known and unknown universes. The path that intersects with our fair city is seldom known to travelers, regardless of their realm. Many stumble upon the city, some are called to it. Even still, others seek it.

Stadarfell presents each of its myriad visitors the same offer; opportunity, growth, knowledge, and freedom. All who visit the dimension find something of value to take back with them to their home world, and some never return at all. Whatever your goal, Stadarfell is at your fingertips; you need only take advantage of it.
